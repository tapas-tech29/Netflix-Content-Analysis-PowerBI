## 📌 Project Overview

The **Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to explore Netflix's global content library. The dashboard provides valuable insights into content growth trends, genre distribution, ratings, country-wise availability, and the balance between Movies and TV Shows.

This project demonstrates practical skills in **Data Cleaning, Data Modeling, DAX Calculations, Data Visualization, and Business Intelligence Reporting**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze Netflix content growth over time
* Compare Movies and TV Shows distribution
* Identify top content-producing countries
* Explore genre and rating trends
* Create an interactive dashboard for business insights

## 💡 Key Insights

* Movies represent the majority of Netflix content.
* Content growth accelerated significantly after 2015.
* The United States contributes the largest number of titles.
* Drama and International content dominate the platform.
* TV Shows continue to grow steadily year after year.
* Netflix content is distributed across multiple countries worldwide.
